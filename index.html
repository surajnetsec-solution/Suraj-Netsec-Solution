// About

Hands-on engineering, not slideware.

I'm Suraj, a network and security engineer with a data center background, now working independently as Suraj Netsec Consulting. Before going independent, my work centered on the systems that keep enterprise networks running and defended — firewalls, VPN tunnels, routing tables, and the NOC/SOC processes that catch problems before they become incidents.

Today I bring that same operational discipline to businesses that don't have — or don't yet need — a full internal security team. Clients get direct access to the engineer doing the work, not a ticket queue.

I work with small businesses, MSPs, and IT managers across India, the US, Europe, and the Middle East, remotely and on a project or retainer basis.
